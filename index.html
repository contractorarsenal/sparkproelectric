Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before you call. Still have questions? Mohammed is happy to talk through your project.

What electrical services does Spark Pro Electric offer?
We specialize in residential and commercial electrical services including panel upgrades, EV charger installation, whole-home surge protection, dedicated circuits, attic and exhaust fan installation, ceiling fan installs, generator hookups, backup battery systems, hot tub wiring, and GFCI outlets. Every install is safe, code-compliant, and done clean.
How do I know if my electrical panel needs an upgrade?
Common signs include frequently tripped breakers, flickering lights, a warm panel, a home over 30 years old, or adding high-demand appliances like an EV charger or hot tub. If your panel is under 100 amps or still uses fuses, it likely needs an upgrade. Call Mohammed and he'll walk you through it over the phone before scheduling anything.
Can I install an EV charger at home, and how long does it take?
Yes, and Spark Pro specializes in this. Most Level 2 EV charger installs take 2 to 4 hours. Mohammed will ask about your panel capacity, garage layout, and EV brand before showing up so he arrives fully equipped. Most jobs are done in a single visit. We handle Tesla Wall Connectors, ChargePoint, JuiceBox, NEMA 14-50 outlets, and all major EV brands.
What are dedicated circuits, and why are they important?
A dedicated circuit powers only one appliance, protecting it from fluctuations caused by other devices. Required for EV chargers, hot tubs, refrigerators, and high-draw equipment. Spark Pro installs dedicated circuits for all home and commercial applications.
Do you handle permits for electrical work?
Yes. Spark Pro handles all permitting for jobs that require it. Clients call this out specifically in their reviews. Every permitted job is inspected and signed off to code, so you have zero issues if you ever sell your home.
